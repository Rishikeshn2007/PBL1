Chronic Kidney Disease Screening Support

CKD Health Assessment

Use this site to enter patient health details and lab values in one organized form. The information should be taken from valid medical records or recent test reports.

Login Dashboard Registration Admin

How to use this site

  1. Click Registration if you are a new user and create your account.
  2. Click Login if you already have an account.
  3. After login, open the patient assessment form.
  4. Enter the patient's age, blood pressure, urine test details, blood chemistry, blood count, and medical history.
  5. Use the exact values from reports where available, including units shown beside each field.
  6. Fill every field marked with a red star because these values are required before submission.
  7. Review the form once, then click Submit. Use Clear Form only when you want to remove all entered values.

Before you begin

  • Keep the patient's blood and urine reports ready.
  • Enter blood pressure in this format: 120/80.
  • Select Yes or No carefully for medical history fields.
  • This site is for assessment support and does not replace advice from a doctor.
